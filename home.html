About Our Company

Welcome to Pighalitadhatu, your trusted die casting company in Karnataka, India. We are a modern die casting manufacturer offering complete die casting services for various industries. Since our beginning in 2025, we have focused on delivering quality aluminium die casting, high pressure die casting, and custom die casting solutions that meet your exact needs.


Our die casting foundry uses the latest technology to create precision die casting parts with perfect finish and strength. Whether you need zinc die casting, magnesium die casting, or low pressure die casting, we have the skills and equipment to help you. We work with both small businesses and large companies across Karnataka and beyond.


At Pighalitadhatu, we understand that every project is different. That is why we offer complete services from design and tooling to finishing and delivery. Get a quote today and experience quality die casting services that make a real difference to your business.
