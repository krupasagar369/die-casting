We work with various die casting alloys to meet diverse application requirements:

Aluminum Alloys
  • A380: Excellent fluidity, most commonly used for general purpose
  • A383: Superior die filling, ideal for complex thin-walled parts
  • ADC12: High strength and corrosion resistance
Zinc Alloys
  • Zamak 3: Excellent balance of strength and ductility
  • Zamak 5: Higher strength with good creep resistance
Magnesium Alloys
  • Lightweight solutions for aerospace and automotive applications

Material Selection Factors: Our engineering team considers strength requirements, weight constraints, corrosion resistance, thermal properties, electrical conductivity, cost considerations, and surface finish requirements to recommend the optimal material for your specific application.
